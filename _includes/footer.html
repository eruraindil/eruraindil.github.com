Privacy Policy

We collect personal information from our users in the regular course of doing business.

what personal information do you collect about me?

When you visit our web site, we also collect the following information about you, through Google Analytics, based on Display Advertising by Google:

  • information about your computer, including your IP address, the type of operating system and browser you use, and your computer's location (coarse-grain)
  • what pages you visit on our site and what links you click on
  • what other sites you've visited recently
  • basic demographic information, including approximate age, gender, and interests

We do not merge personally-identifiable information with non-personally identifiable information collected.

how do you use this information?

  • customer service
  • marketing
  • product improvement

We will also disclose your personal information if we are required by law to do so.

how do you get my consent?

By using this site, you are consenting to automatic collection of your information by Google Analytics.

Saying no is called "opting out." By opting out, you can tell us not to collect the information and/or not to share it with others.

how do I opt out?

If you provide us with information about your preferences and opinions and you do not want that information to be used for marketing purposes please contact us at the address below.

if you would not like our website to collect information about you:

  • You may opt out of Google Analytics for Display Advertising by using Google's Ads Settings, and choosing to Opt out of interest-based ads on Google and across the web.
  • You may opt out of Google Analytics reporting entirely by following the instructions at Google's Analytics Opt-Out page.

how do I get more information?

Please contact me, via the contact page.
