Matthew lives in Saskatoon, Saskatchewan, works in the technology department at the Lung Association of Saskatchewan, and is in search of some community theatre that would let him play with fancy lighting and sound equipment.

Matthew is from a small town in Nova Scotia and has been playing around with technology most of his life. He attended King's-Edgehill School and was involved with the theatre programme as a technician.

After completing high school, Matthew spent a year at St. Francis Xavier University, obtained his first year of a Bachelor of Information Systems (BIS) with Dean's List, honours placement before deciding that StFX and the BIS program wasn't quite right for him. He transfered to the University of Guelph and combined his interests in psychology, computer science and economics in the Information Systems and Human Behaviour (Honours) major (ISHB), Economics minor.

Between school years at StFX and the University of Guelph, he spent a summer working in professional theatre at the Ship's Company Theatre in Parrsboro, Nova Scotia, as assistant technical director, putting his knowledge of theatre technology from high school to use in a professional capacity.

At the University of Guelph, Matthew spent his time working at Student Volunteer Connections of Guelph, a student run volunteer centre, as their webmaster and IT manager; and at Curtain Call Productions as a technician and Technical Director.
